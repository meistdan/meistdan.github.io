Biography

Hi, I'm Daniel, a researcher at the Czech Technical University, where I've recently received a Ph.D. degree advised by Prof. Jiří Bittner. I'm fascinated all about ray tracing based rendering including real-time ray tracing, acceleration data structures, and global illumination.

Research Interests

  • Data Structures for Ray Tracing
  • Real-time Ray Tracing
  • Global Illumination
  • Parallel Computing and GPGPU
